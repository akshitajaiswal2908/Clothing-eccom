Refund Policy

Last updated: 16 August 2025

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn’t right, this page explains how returns, exchanges, and refunds work at Clothing‑Eccom.

Returns

Our return window lasts 30 days from the date of delivery. If 30 days have passed since your purchase, we can’t offer a refund or exchange.

  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in the original packaging with tags attached.
  • A receipt or proof of purchase is required.
  • Please do not send your purchase back to the manufacturer.

Non‑returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable or digital products
  • Intimate or sanitary goods (e.g., underwear, lingerie, swimwear bottoms, socks once opened)
  • Health and personal care items
  • Hazardous materials, flammable liquids or gases
  • Perishables (e.g., food, flowers, newspapers, magazines)

Partial Refunds (if applicable)

  • Books with obvious signs of use
  • Opened media/software (CD, DVD, cassette, vinyl, video game, software)
  • Any item not in original condition, damaged, or missing parts for reasons not due to our error
  • Any item returned more than 30 days after delivery (only in exceptional cases and at our discretion)

Refunds (if applicable)

Once your return is received and inspected, we’ll email you to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, we’ll process the refund to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, this may take 5–10 business days after we issue the refund.

Late or Missing Refunds

  • Check your bank account again.
  • Contact your card company; it may take time before a refund is officially posted.
  • Contact your bank; processing times can vary.
  • If you’ve done all of this and still haven’t received your refund, email us at support@clothing-eccom.com.

Sale Items

Only regular‑priced items may be refunded. Sale or discounted items are not refundable unless required by law.

Exchanges (if applicable)

We replace items only if they are defective or damaged. To request an exchange for the same item:

  • Email: support@clothing-eccom.com
  • Ship to: 622 Manglam Electronic Market, Jaipur, Rajasthan, India 302001

Gifts

  • If the item was marked as a gift and shipped directly to you, you’ll receive a gift credit for the value of your return. Once received, a gift certificate will be emailed/mailed to you.
  • If the item wasn’t marked as a gift or the order was shipped to the gift giver, the refund will be sent to the gift giver.

Return Shipping

  • Mail your product to: Returns – Clothing‑Eccom, 622 Manglam Electronic Market, Jaipur, Rajasthan, India 302001.
  • You are responsible for paying your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non‑refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping may be deducted from your refund.
  • Transit times vary by location.
  • For items valued over ₹6,000, consider using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We can’t guarantee that we’ll receive your returned item.

Need Help?

Questions about returns, exchanges, or refunds? Contact us at support@clothing-eccom.com.
