Shipping Policy

Last updated: 16 August 2025

Processing vs. Shipping: Order processing time is separate from the shipping time shown at checkout.

All orders are processed within 7–8 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after you receive your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Delays may occur during high‑volume periods, courier disruptions, or events outside our control.

Domestic Shipping (India)

We currently ship across India. Shipping charges are calculated and displayed at checkout.

Shipping option Estimated delivery time Price (INR)
Standard Delivery 7–8 business days ₹100
Economy Delivery 8–12 business days ₹70
Budget Delivery 12–25 business days ₹50

Tip: We occasionally offer free shipping promotions (e.g., on orders over ₹999). Any active offer will be shown at checkout.

Local Delivery (Indore)

  • Free local delivery on orders over ₹999 within Indore city limits.
  • Orders under ₹999 incur a ₹50 local delivery fee.
  • Deliveries are made between 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM on business days.
  • We will notify you via SMS on the day of delivery using the phone number provided at checkout.

Need to confirm if your address is within our local delivery range? Contact support and share your PIN code.

In‑Store Pickup

  • Free pickup is available at our Indore location.
  • After selecting Local Pickup at checkout, your order will be ready within 2–3 business days.
  • We will email you when your order is ready, including pickup instructions.
  • Pickup hours: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.
  • Please bring your order confirmation email when you arrive.

International Shipping

We currently ship to USA, UK, Canada, and Australia. Shipping charges are calculated at checkout.

Shipping option Estimated delivery time Price (INR)
Standard International 10–20 business days ₹800
Express International 7–12 business days ₹1200
Priority International 5–8 business days ₹2000

We do not ship to certain restricted countries and regions at this time.

Your order may be subject to import duties and taxes (including VAT) once it reaches the destination country. These charges are the customer’s responsibility and are not covered by Clothing‑Eccom.

Questions?

If you have any questions about shipping or delivery, contact us at support@clothing-eccom.com.
